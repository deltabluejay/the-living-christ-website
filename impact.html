His Impact

Because of Christ, everyone who has and will live has the chance to return to live with Him and Heavenly Father again. His atoning sacrifice and resurrection is the single most important thing to have ever happened in the history of the world. Because of Him, people across the world with vast cultural differences can be united as children of God with a shared faith in Christ and love for each other. Through Christ, we can all become better people- we can be more patient, more kind, more loving, and more understanding. Christ's love for me in all of my imperfection and inadequacy inspires me to want to love others like He does. Focusing on Christ helps me see others the way He does and want to comfort those that are struggling. I have a testimony that Christ is our loving Savior and has been and is always there to teach and bless those that are willing to hear His message. He wants us to turn to Him so that we may receive eternal life and happiness.
