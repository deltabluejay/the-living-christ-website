Christ's Pre-Mortal Ministry

In His pre-mortal ministry, Christ prepared His people for His coming by teaching the prophets of old about Him. They testified of His divine purpose, His coming, and what He would perform while on Earth to establish His gospel.

Isaiah

And it shall come to pass in the last days, that the mountain of the Lord's house shall be established in the top of the mountains, and shall be exalted above the hills; and all nations shall flow unto it.

Isaiah 2:2

Therefore the Lord himself shall give you a sign; Behold, a virgin shall conceive, and bear a son, and shall call his name Immanuel.

Isaiah 7:14

2 Nephi 26:33

And because that I have spoken one word ye need not suppose that I cannot speak another; for my work is not yet finished; neither shall it be until the end of man, neither from that time henceforth and forever.

Prophets of both the New and Old World looked forward with faith for Christ's coming. They diligently followed God's commandments and had faith in why what Christ would do when He came was so important to their salvation. They prepared the way for Him to come and establish His gospel.
