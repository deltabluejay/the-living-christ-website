Christ's Post-Mortal Ministry

The influence of Christ extends to our time today. Through Joseph Smith He restored the true Church and the fullness of His gospel. He directs our prophet, President Neslon, today. We can hear Christ through the voice of our leaders and through each other as we tune ourselves to the Spirit.

Christ is the central tenet of our religion. Only through Him are we able to be saved. By learning about Him and coming to understand Him better, we will become closer to Him, become more like Him, and ultimately receive forgiveness for our sins and receive eternal life.
